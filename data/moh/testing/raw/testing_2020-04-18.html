COVID-19 - Testing rates for ethnicity and DHB

Page last updated 24 April 2020

On this page:

  • Test rate by ethnicity
  • Test rate by DHB of residence
  • Test rate by DHB and ethnicity

A high number of tests across the country helps identify cases of COVID-19 early, manage clusters of cases when we find them and track how well our efforts are working.This page records information about the ethnicity breakdown of testing and testing by region. It is updated weekly.

Localised testing helps ensure we are both looking for and finding cases across the country.

Surveillance testing - testing of a wider population to make sure there are not undetected cases - is also being carried out in parts of New Zealand.

If you are looking for information about how testing works or what might happen if you need a test, see the COVID-19 website.
